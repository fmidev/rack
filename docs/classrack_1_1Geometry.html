Detailed Description

Distances in meters, angles in radians.

  • : Earth radius, the distance from the Earth's center to the radar
  • : beam distance; the distance from the radar to the bin
  • : distance from the Earth's center to the bin
  • : altitude from ground to beam point
  • : ground distance; surface distance from the radar to the ground point

eta : beam elevation

  • alpha : "sky angle",
  • beta : "ground angle"
  • gamma : "radar angle"
  • r = radian
  • d = degree
